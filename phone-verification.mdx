---
title: 'Phone Number Verification'
description: 'Verify phone numbers with MailGreet single and bulk validation to ensure accurate contact data and improve SMS deliverability'
---

**MailGreet** offers you the facility to verify the phone numbers.

## Verify Single Phone Number 

To verify a single phone number:-

1. Navigate to the [Verify](https://mailgreet.com/dashboard/verify) page.

<img
  className="block dark:hidden"
  src="/images/verify-page-light.png"
  alt="Verify Page Light"
/>
<img
  className="hidden dark:block"
  src="/images/verify-page-dark.png"
  alt="Verify Page Dark"
/>

2. Click on the **Phone Verification** tab.
3. In the **Single Phone Validation** section, enter the phone number you want to verify in the given input field.
4. Click on the **Validate** button next to it.

<Info>

**Note**: **1 credit** is used per 1 phone number validation.

</Info>

5. You will see the validation details below the input text field in which you enter the phone number.

<img
  className="block dark:hidden"
  src="/images/verify-single-phone-number-light.png"
  alt="Verify Single Phone Number Light"
/>
<img
  className="hidden dark:block"
  src="/images/verify-single-phone-number-dark.png"
  alt="Verify Single Phone Number Dark"
/>

6. Or, you can also see the validation report in the **Recent Validations** section at the bottom of the page.
7. The latest entry will be the topmost entry.

<img
  className="block dark:hidden"
  src="/images/recent-phone-validation-report-light.png"
  alt="Recent Phone Validation Report Light"
/>
<img
  className="hidden dark:block"
  src="/images/recent-phone-validation-report-dark.png"
  alt="Recent Phone Validation Report Dark"
/>

8. Now, you have successfully validated the single phone number.

## Verify Multiple Phone Numbers 

To verify multiple phone numbers at once:-

1. Navigate to the [Verify](https://mailgreet.com/dashboard/verify) page.

<img
  className="block dark:hidden"
  src="/images/verify-page-light.png"
  alt="Verify Page Light"
/>
<img
  className="hidden dark:block"
  src="/images/verify-page-dark.png"
  alt="Verify Page Dark"
/>

2. Click on the **Phone Verification** tab.
3. There are **two ways** to do **multiple phone numbers verification**.
4. First, under **Bulk Upload** section, upload the .csv file containing multiple phone numbers. 

<img
  className="block dark:hidden"
  src="/images/bulk-phone-verification-light.png"
  alt="Bulk Phone Verification Light"
/>
<img
  className="hidden dark:block"
  src="/images/bulk-phone-verification-dark.png"
  alt="Bulk Phone Verification Dark"
/>

5. Click the **Validate** button in front of the uploaded file.
6. The file verification will occur and will take a few moments.
7. You can see the file verification progress under the **Recent Validations**.

<img
  className="block dark:hidden"
  src="/images/recent-validations-light.png"
  alt="Recent Validations Light"
/>
<img
  className="hidden dark:block"
  src="/images/recent-validations-dark.png"
  alt="Recent Validations Dark"
/>

8. Click on the report in the **Recent Validations** section to see the **Validation Summary** dialogbox.
9. The dialogbox contains the phone numbers validation report.

<img
  className="block dark:hidden"
  src="/images/bulk-phone-verification-summary-light.png"
  alt="Bulk Phone Verification Summary Light"
/>
<img
  className="hidden dark:block"
  src="/images/bulk-phone-verification-summary-dark.png"
  alt="Bulk Phone Verification Summary Dark"
/>

10. You can also download the report by clicking on the **Download Reports** button.
11. You will get three options, **Valid Only**, **Invalid Only**, **Download All**.
12. Choose the appropriate option and the download will automatically started.
13. Or, you can click on the **Download** button under the **Actions** field.
14. You will get five options such as **Valid Only**, **Valid and Catch-All**, **Invalid Only**, **Invalid and Unknown**, **Download All**.

<img
  className="block dark:hidden"
  src="/images/download-bulk-phone-verification-report-light.png"
  alt="Download Bulk Phone Verification Report Light"
/>
<img
  className="hidden dark:block"
  src="/images/download-bulk-phone-verification-report-dark.png"
  alt="Download Bulk Phone Verification Report Dark"
/>

15. Second, under the **Or paste phone numbers** section, paste multiple phone numbers for verification separated by spaces, commas and newlines.

<img
  className="block dark:hidden"
  src="/images/paste-phone-numbers-light.png"
  alt="Paste Phone Numbers Light"
/>
<img
  className="hidden dark:block"
  src="/images/paste-phone-numbers-dark.png"
  alt="Paste Phone Numbers Dark"
/>

16. The **Validate Pasted Numbers** button will be activated. 
17. Click on it to validate multiple phone numbers at once.
18. The phone verification will occur and will take a few moments.
19. You can see the phone verification progress under the **Recent Validations**.

<img
  className="block dark:hidden"
  src="/images/recent-validations-pasted-phone-numbers-light.png"
  alt="Recent Validations Pasted Phone Numbers Light"
/>
<img
  className="hidden dark:block"
  src="/images/recent-validations-pasted-phone-numbers-dark.png"
  alt="Recent Validations Pasted Phone Numbers Dark"
/>

20. You can click on the latest entry in the **Recent Validations** section to check the report.
21. A **Validation Summary** dialog box will appear.

<img
  className="block dark:hidden"
  src="/images/pasted-phone-validation-summary-light.png"
  alt="Pasted Phone Validation Summary Light"
/>
<img
  className="hidden dark:block"
  src="/images/pasted-phone-validation-summary-dark.png"
  alt="Pasted Phone Validation Summary Dark"
/>

22. You can either click on the **Download Reports** button or on the **Download** button of the respective pasted phone numbers list below the **Actions** field to download the report according to several options that you will get when you click these respective buttons.
23. After clicking the button, your reports will get downloaded automatically.
24. Now, you have successfully validate multiple phone numbers at once using two methods.
