# Engagement Metrics

Understanding subscriber engagement helps you identify your most valuable contacts and optimize your email strategy.

---

## What is Engagement?

Email engagement measures how subscribers interact with your campaigns. High engagement indicates:
- Your content is relevant and valuable
- You're reaching the right audience
- Your email timing and frequency are appropriate

---

## Analyzing Engagement

### Most Engaged Subscribers

Identify your top performers:
- View subscribers sorted by engagement score
- Segment highly engaged contacts for special offers
- Use as seed for lookalike audiences
- Reward loyal subscribers

### Inactive Subscribers

Identify subscribers who need re-engagement:
- No opens in 90+ days
- No clicks in 180+ days
- Consider re-engagement campaigns or removal

<Warning>
Keeping inactive subscribers hurts deliverability. Clean your list regularly!
</Warning>

## Improving Engagement

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Improve Open Rates" icon="envelope-open">
    - Write compelling subject lines (A/B test them!)
    - Use personalization in subject and preview text
    - Optimize send times for your audience
    - Build sender reputation with consistent quality
    - Ensure from name is recognizable
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Boost Click Rates" icon="mouse-pointer">
    - Make CTAs clear and prominent
    - Use buttons instead of text links
    - Limit to one primary action per email
    - Create urgency when appropriate
    - Make content scannable
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Overall Engagement" icon="chart-line">
    - Send relevant, segmented content
    - Maintain consistent sending schedule
    - Provide genuine value in every email
    - Remove inactive subscribers
    - Test and iterate continuously
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Engagement-Based Segmentation

Create segments based on engagement:

| Segment | Criteria | Use Case |
|---------|----------|----------|
| **VIPs** | Score 80+ | Exclusive offers, early access |
| **Engaged** | Score 50-79 | Regular communications |
| **At Risk** | Score 20-49 | Re-engagement campaign |
| **Dormant** | Score <20 | Win-back or remove |

## Re-Engagement Strategies

For inactive subscribers:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Identify Inactives">
    Segment subscribers with low scores or no recent activity
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send Re-engagement Campaign">
    "We miss you" email with special offer or value
  </Step>
  <Step title="Give One More Chance">
    Final notice: "Do you want to keep hearing from us?"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clean Your List">
    Remove those who don't respond after 2-3 attempts
  </Step>
</Steps>
