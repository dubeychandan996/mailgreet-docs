# Email Verification

Ensure your emails reach real inboxes by verifying email addresses before sending campaigns. MailGreet's email verification helps you maintain a clean list, reduce bounce rates, and protect your sender reputation.

---

## Why Verify Emails?

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Reduce Bounces" icon="triangle-exclamation">
    Invalid emails cause hard bounces, hurting your sender reputation
  </Card>
  <Card title="Save Credits" icon="coins">
    Don't waste email sends on addresses that don't exist
  </Card>
  <Card title="Improve Deliverability" icon="inbox">
    Clean lists mean better inbox placement rates
  </Card>
  <Card title="Protect Reputation" icon="shield-check">
    Avoid spam traps and blacklists that damage your domain
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
When you add new subscribers through the platform, their emails are **automatically verified** at no extra cost. The verification feature is for validating external lists or individual addresses.
</Note>

---

## Verification Results Explained

After verification, each email receives a status:

| Status | Meaning | Recommendation |
|--------|---------|----------------|
| ✅ **Valid** | Email exists and can receive mail | Safe to send |
| ⚠️ **Catch-All** | Domain accepts all emails (can't confirm specific address) | Send with caution |
| ❌ **Invalid** | Email doesn't exist or is malformed | Do not send |
| ❓ **Unknown** | Unable to verify (server timeout, etc.) | Verify again later |

  <Step title="Select Email Verification">
    Click on the **Email Verification** tab if not already selected

  <Step title="Enter Email Address">

  <Step title="Click Validate">

    <img
      className="block dark:hidden rounded-lg shadow-md my-4"

Verify hundreds or thousands of emails at once using file upload or paste methods.

    In the **Bulk Upload** section, drag and drop your CSV/TXT file or click to browse

      alt="Bulk Email Upload"
    />

  <Step title="Start Validation">

    <img
      className="block dark:hidden rounded-lg shadow-md my-4"

      className="hidden dark:block rounded-lg shadow-md my-4"

## Best Practices

<Tip>
**Verify before importing:** Always verify external email lists before adding them to your subscriber base to maintain list hygiene.
</Tip>

<Warning>
**Remove invalid emails:** Sending to invalid addresses damages your sender reputation. Always remove emails marked as "Invalid" from your campaigns.
</Warning>

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Regular Cleaning" icon="broom">
    Verify your list every 3-6 months to catch emails that have become invalid
  </Card>
  <Card title="Focus on Valid" icon="bullseye">
    Prioritize "Valid" emails in campaigns. Use "Catch-All" with caution.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
