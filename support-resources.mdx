### Email Support

<Card title="support@mailgreet.com" icon="envelope" href="mailto:support@mailgreet.com">

**When emailing support, please include:**
- Your account email address
- Detailed description of your issue
- Screenshots if relevant
- Steps you've already tried
- Any error messages you're seeing

## Quick Help Topics

Click any topic below for immediate guidance:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I can't log into my account" icon="lock">
    1. Verify you're using the correct email address
    2. Click "Forgot Password" to reset your password
    3. Check your spam folder for the reset email
    4. Clear your browser cookies and try again
    5. If still stuck, email support@mailgreet.com
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="My emails aren't being delivered" icon="envelope">
    1. Check your sender domain is verified
    2. Run email verification on your subscriber list
    3. Review your content for spam triggers
    4. Check your sending limits haven't been reached
    5. See our [Common Issues](/common-issues) guide for more help
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="I need to upgrade/downgrade my plan" icon="credit-card">
    1. Go to Settings → Billing
    2. Click "Change Plan"
    3. Select your new plan
    4. Confirm the change
    5. See [Billing & Plans](/billing-and-plans) for details
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="I need to cancel my subscription" icon="circle-xmark">
    1. Go to Settings → Billing
    2. Click "Cancel Subscription"
    3. Complete the cancellation flow
    4. Access continues until end of billing period
    5. Data retained for 30 days after cancellation
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="I've hit my sending limits" icon="gauge-high">
    1. Wait until limits reset (monthly)
    2. Upgrade to a higher plan for more emails
    3. Contact support for temporary limit increase (Enterprise)
    4. Review if all sends are necessary
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
