## Viewing Your Current Plan

1. Go to **Settings → Billing**
2. View your current plan details including:
   - Plan name and price
   - Renewal date
   - Subscriber limit and usage
   - Email sending limit and usage
   - Verification credits remaining

## Upgrading Your Plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Billing">
    Navigate to **Settings → Billing**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Upgrade">
    Select the **Upgrade Plan** button
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose New Plan">
    Compare plans and select the one that fits your needs
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Payment">
    Review the price difference and confirm
  </Step>
</Steps>

**What happens when you upgrade:**
- New limits take effect immediately
- You're charged a prorated amount for the current billing period
- Future renewals are at the new plan price

---

## Downgrading Your Plan

<Warning>
Before downgrading, ensure your current usage is within the limits of the lower plan. You may need to remove subscribers or reduce usage first.
</Warning>

1. Go to **Settings → Billing**
2. Click **Change Plan**
3. Select a lower-tier plan
4. Review impact on your features and limits
5. Confirm the downgrade

The change takes effect at the end of your current billing period.

## Managing Payment Methods

### Adding a Payment Method

1. Go to **Settings → Billing → Payment Methods**
2. Click **Add Payment Method**
3. Enter your card details
4. Save the payment method

### Updating Payment Information

Keep your payment method current to avoid service interruption:
- Update expiring cards before they expire
- Set a default payment method
- Remove old or unused cards

## Purchasing Verification Credits

Need more email or phone verification credits?

## Canceling Your Subscription

To cancel your MailGreet subscription:

1. Go to **Settings → Billing**
2. Click **Cancel Subscription**
3. Provide feedback (optional but appreciated)
4. Confirm cancellation

**What happens when you cancel:**
- You keep access until end of billing period
- Your data is retained for 30 days
- You can reactivate anytime during the retention period

<Note>
If you're canceling due to an issue, please contact support first. We'd love to help resolve any problems!
</Note>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="When am I billed?">
    Subscriptions are billed monthly on the anniversary of your signup date. You'll receive an email before each charge.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What happens if payment fails?">
    We'll retry the payment and send you an email. If payment fails multiple times, your account may be limited until payment is resolved.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I get a refund?">
    We offer refunds within 14 days of payment for unused services. Contact support for refund requests.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Is there a discount for annual billing?">
    Yes! Annual plans offer up to 20% discount compared to monthly billing. Contact sales for details.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
