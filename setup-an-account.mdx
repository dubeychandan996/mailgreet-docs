# Setup Your MailGreet Account

Follow these simple steps to create your MailGreet account and get ready to start your email marketing journey.

## Create Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit MailGreet">
    Go to the [MailGreet website](https://mailgreet.com/) and click the **Sign up free** button at the top of the page.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Your Details">
    Under the **Create your account** form:
    - Enter your **Full name**
    - Enter your **Email address**
    - Create a secure **Password**
    - Accept the **Terms of Service** and **Privacy Policy**
    - Click **Continue**

    <img
      className="block dark:hidden rounded-lg shadow-md my-4"
      src="/images/signup-step1-light.png"
      alt="Signup Step 1"
    />
    <img
      className="hidden dark:block rounded-lg shadow-md my-4"
      src="/images/signup-step1-dark.png"
      alt="Signup Step 1"
    />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Tell Us About Your Business">
    On the **Tell us about your business** form:
    - Enter your **Company name**
    - Select your **Company size** from the dropdown
    - Choose your **Industry** from the dropdown
    - Click **Continue**

    <img
      className="block dark:hidden rounded-lg shadow-md my-4"
      src="/images/signup-step2-light.png"
      alt="Signup Step 2"
    />
    <img
      className="hidden dark:block rounded-lg shadow-md my-4"
      src="/images/signup-step2-dark.png"
      alt="Signup Step 2"
    />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Your Goals">
    On the **What are your goals?** form:
    - Select one or more options for what you want to achieve with MailGreet
    - Click **Get started**

    <img
      className="block dark:hidden rounded-lg shadow-md my-4"

    <img

      alt="Signup Step 3"
    />
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Confirm Your Email

<Note>
After completing the signup:
1. Check your email inbox for a confirmation message from MailGreet
2. Click the **Confirm your email** link in the email
3. You'll be redirected to your new MailGreet dashboard

**Don't see the email?** Check your spam folder or [contact support](mailto:support@mailgreet.com).
</Note>
