## Setup an Account

### How to sign up for MailGreet

1. Go to the website [MailGreet website](https://mailgreet.com/).
2. At the top of the homepage, click the button [Sign up free](https://mailgreet.com/signup).
3. Then on Signup page, under **Create your account** form, enter the **Full name**.

  className="block dark:hidden"
  src="/images/signup-step1-light.png"

/>
<img

4. Enter the **Email address**.
5. Enter the **Password**.
6. Agree to the **Terms of Service** and **Privacy Policy** of the website by clicking on the checkbox.
7. Click on the **Continue** button.
8. On the next webpage, Under **Tell us about your business** form, enter **Company name**.

<img
  className="block dark:hidden"
  src="/images/signup-step2-light.png"
  alt="Hero Light"
/>
<img
  className="hidden dark:block"
  src="/images/signup-step2-dark.png"
  alt="Hero Dark"
/>

9. Choose the **Company Size** from the dropdown menu.
10. Choose the **Industry** from the dropdown menu.
11. Click on the **Continue** button.
12. On the next webpage, Under **What are your goals?** form, select one or more checkboxes for **What do you want to achieve with MailGreet?**.

<img
  className="block dark:hidden"
  src="/images/signup-step3-light.png"
  alt="Hero Light"
/>
<img
  className="hidden dark:block"
  src="/images/signup-step3-dark.png"
  alt="Hero Dark"
/>

13. Click on the **Get started** button.

### Account Confirmation

1. After the signup process, an account confirmation email is sent to your email account.
2. Please confirm the signup by clicking on the **Confirm your mail** link in that mail.
3. A new webpage is opened with the MailGreet homepage.

Alternatively, you can also **Signup using Google Account**.

Now, your Account Signup process is complete.
