## Export Subscribers

1. Navigate to the [Subscribers](https://mailgreet.com/dashboard/subscribers) page.

<img
  className="block dark:hidden"
  src="/images/imported-subscribers-light.png"
  alt="Exported Subscribers Light"
/>
<img
  className="hidden dark:block"
  src="/images/imported-subscribers-dark.png"
  alt="Exported Subscribers Dark"
/>

2. Click on the **Export** button present at the top below the **Search** field.

<img
  className="block dark:hidden"
  src="/images/import-button-light.png"
  alt="Export Button Light"
/>
<img
  className="hidden dark:block"
  src="/images/import-button-dark.png"
  alt="Export Button Dark"
/>

3. You will see the subscribers list got downloaded automatically in the **.csv format** file.
