# Subscriber Growth

Monitor the health of your subscriber list with growth analytics. Understanding how your list grows and changes helps you make better marketing decisions.

---

## Accessing Growth Analytics

1. Navigate to **Analytics** in the sidebar
2. Click on **Subscriber Growth** or view growth metrics on your dashboard
3. Select date ranges to analyze specific periods

## Churn Analysis

Monitor why subscribers leave:

### Unsubscribe Reasons

When subscribers opt out, analyze the reasons:

| Reason | Action to Take |
|--------|----------------|
| Too many emails | Reduce sending frequency |
| Content not relevant | Improve segmentation |
| Never signed up | Add double opt-in |
| Changed email | Normal churn, no action needed |

### Calculating Churn Rate

```
Monthly Churn Rate = (Unsubscribes + Bounces) / Total Subscribers Ã— 100
```

<Info>
Healthy churn rates are typically under 2% per month. Higher rates indicate problems to address.
</Info>

## List Health Score

Your overall list health considers:

- **Bounce rate**: Keep under 2%
- **Unsubscribe rate**: Keep under 0.5% per campaign
- **Spam complaints**: Keep under 0.1%
- **Engagement**: Active opens and clicks
- **Email validity**: Verified addresses

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Excellent">
    - Bounce rate: <1%
    - High engagement (>30% opens)
    - Regular list cleaning
    - Growing steadily
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Good">
    - Bounce rate: 1-2%
    - Moderate engagement (15-30% opens)
    - Occasional list cleaning
    - Slow growth
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Needs Attention">
    - Bounce rate: >2%
    - Low engagement (<15% opens)
    - List not cleaned recently
    - Stagnant or declining
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Improving List Growth

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Organic Growth Strategies" icon="seedling">
    - Add signup forms to your website
    - Create compelling lead magnets
    - Run social media campaigns
    - Partner with complementary businesses
    - Encourage referrals from existing subscribers
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Reduce Unsubscribes" icon="user-check">
    - Send relevant, valuable content
    - Let subscribers choose email frequency
    - Segment for personalized content
    - Don't email too frequently
    - Make unsubscribe easy (reduces spam complaints)
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Improve List Quality" icon="star">
    - Use double opt-in
    - Verify emails before adding
    - Remove inactive subscribers
    - Never buy email lists
    - Regular list cleaning
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
