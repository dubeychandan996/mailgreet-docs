## Groups

In MailGreet, Groups are custom lists you create to organize your contacts based on shared interests or characteristics. Using groups allows you to send targeted emails to specific segments of your audience, improving relevance and engagement.

<Info>

**Note**: In other email marketing platforms, groups may also be referred to as **tags**, **lists**, or **audiences**.

</Info>

## Create a Group

1. Navigate to the [Subscribers](https://mailgreet.com/dashboard/subscribers) page.

5. Provide the group name.

## Segments 

At **MailGreet**, a segment is a dynamic list of subscribers filtered by specific rules you set. Use our powerful filtering tools to create segments based on subscriber data like groups, custom fields, signup date, location, campaign activity, automation workflows, inactivity, and more.

Once you define your rule(s), MailGreet instantly shows you which subscribers match—giving you a clear view of your segment’s size and who’s in it. It’s an easy way to target the right people with the right message.

## Benefits of Segments

Create targeted subscriber segments based on:
1. Demographic data
2. Engagement metrics (opens, clicks)
3. Purchase history
4. Custom field values
5. Website activity

## Create a Segment

To create a segment:-

1. Navigate to the [Subscribers](https://mailgreet.com/dashboard/subscribers) page.
2. Click on the **Segments** tab.

<img
  className="block dark:hidden"
  src="/images/segment-tab-light.png"
  alt="Segment Tab Light"
/>
<img
  className="hidden dark:block"
  src="/images/segment-tab-dark.png"
  alt="Segment Tab Dark"
/>

3. Click on the **Create new segment** button in the **Segments** section or Click on the **Create segment** button in the **Create Your First Segment** section. 

<Info>

**Note**: The **Create segment** button in the **Create Your First Segment** section will be disappeared when you click on the segment in the **Segments** section.

</Info>

4. A dialog box will appear.

<img
  className="block dark:hidden"
  src="/images/create-new-segment-dialogbox-light.png"
  alt="Create New Segment Dialogbox Light"
/>
<img
  className="hidden dark:block"
  src="/images/create-new-segment-dialogbox-dark.png"
  alt="Create New Segment Dialogbox Dark"
/>

5. Enter the **Segment name**, **Description**, **Conditions** to segment the subscribers.
6. Segment Conditions can be based upon **system default fields** such as **Email**, **First Name**, **Last Name**, **Status**, **Location**, **Timezone**, **Source**, **Created At**, **Updated At**, **Last Login**, **Opt-in Date**, **Email Status**.
7. Or, you can segment subscribers according to your own **custom defined fields**. For that, you have to first create those custom fields before they are shown in the **Conditions** section of the **Create new segment** dialogbox below the **system default fields**.
8. You can also add **Logical Conditions** on the conditions using **All (AND)** or **Any (OR)**.
9. You can add multiple conditions as you want to segment the subscribers.
10. To add multiple conditions, simply click on the **Add another condition** button.
11. Specify the new condition.
12. After specifying all the conditions, click on the **Create segment** button.
13. You have successfully created a new segment for the subscribers.
14. Subscribers will automatically get filtered according to this new segment that you have just created.
15. The created segment will be shown in the **Segments** section.

<img
  className="block dark:hidden"
  src="/images/created-segment-light.png"
  alt="Created Segment Light"
/>
<img
  className="hidden dark:block"
  src="/images/created-segment-dark.png"
  alt="Created Segment Dark"
/>

15. The number of subscribers which gets filtered according to this new segment will be reflected in front of this newly created segment.
16. Click on the segment to know more about the number of subscribers it have.

<img
  className="block dark:hidden"
  src="/images/segment-subscribers-light.png"
  alt="Segment Subscribers Light"
/>
<img
  className="hidden dark:block"
  src="/images/segment-subscribers-dark.png"
  alt="Segment Subscribers Dark"
/>

17. The old subscribers are successfully and automatically filtered or segmented according to your newly created segment.

<Info>

**Note**: The new subscribers in the future will automatically gets segmented or filtered according to the segments created.

</Info>
