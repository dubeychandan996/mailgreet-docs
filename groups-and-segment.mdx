## Groups

In MailGreet, Groups are custom lists you create to organize your contacts based on shared interests or characteristics. Using groups allows you to send targeted emails to specific segments of your audience, improving relevance and engagement.

<Info>

**Note**: In other email marketing platforms, groups may also be referred to as **tags**, **lists**, or **audiences**.

</Info>

## Create a Group

To create a group:- 

1. Navigate to the [Subscribers](https://mailgreet.com/dashboard/subscribers) page.
2. Click on the Groups tab.
3. Click on the **Create new group** button.
4. A dialog box will appear.
5. Provide the group name.
6. Also provide the group description.
7. Click on the **Create Group** button.
