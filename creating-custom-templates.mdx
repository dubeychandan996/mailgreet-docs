## What is an Email Template?

An email template is a pre-designed layout that serves as the foundation for your email campaigns. Templates in MailGreet help you:

- Create consistent branding across all your emails
- Save time by reusing designs
- Ensure mobile responsiveness
- Maintain professional design without coding skills

## Create Custom Template

To create a custom template:-

1. Navigate to the [Templates](https://mailgreet.com/dashboard/templates) page.

<img
  className="block dark:hidden"
  src="/images/templates-page-light.png"
  alt="Templates Page Light"
/>
<img
  className="hidden dark:block"
  src="/images/templates-page-dark.png"
  alt="Templates Page Dark"
/>

2. Click on the **Create template** button located above the search templates bar in the top right corner.
3. Or, you can click on the **Create new template** section right below the **All Templates** tab.
4. **New template** page will open.

<img
  className="block dark:hidden"
  src="/images/new-template-light.png"
  alt="New Template Light"
/>
<img
  className="hidden dark:block"
  src="/images/new-template-dark.png"
  alt="New Template Dark"
/>

5. Enter the **Template name**.
6. Select the type of template either **Campaign** (Selected by default) or **Landing Page**.
7. Click on **Save and continue** button.
8. The template editor page will open.

<img
  className="block dark:hidden"
  src="/images/template-editor-light.png"
  alt="Template Editor Light"
/>
<img
  className="hidden dark:block"
  src="/images/template-editor-dark.png"
  alt="Template Editor Dark"
/>

9. Built and customize the template according to your own needs and personalization.
10. From the left sidebar of the template editor webpage, you can select and insert as many elements as you want such as **Heading**, **Text**, **Image**, **Button**, **Divider**, **Sections**, **Gallery**, **Footer**, etc.
11. After building and customizing the template, click on the **Save Template** button at the topright corner of webpage.
12. Now, you have successfully created a custom template.
13. Your custom templates will be shown in the **All Templates** tab.


## Export Template

To export a template:-

1. Navigate to the [Templates](https://mailgreet.com/dashboard/templates) page.

<img
  className="block dark:hidden"
  src="/images/templates-page-light.png"
  alt="Templates Page Light"
/>
<img
  className="hidden dark:block"
  src="/images/templates-page-dark.png"
  alt="Templates Page Dark"
/>

2. Click on any template which you want to export.
3. The template editor page will open.
4. Click on the **Export** button below the **Template Actions**.

<img
  className="block dark:hidden"
  src="/images/export-button-light.png"
  alt="Templates Page Light"
/>
<img
  className="hidden dark:block"
  src="/images/export-button-dark.png"
  alt="Templates Page Dark"
/>


5. Your template will be downloaded automatically.
6. The template will be in .json format.
7. Now, you have successfully exported the template.
