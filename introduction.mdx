## Quick Tour

### Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard is the command center for your email marketing. At a glance, you can see:

<img
  className="rounded-lg shadow-md my-4"
  src="/images/screenshots/dashboard.png"
  alt="MailGreet Dashboard Overview"
/>

- **Total Subscribers**: Your current list size with growth percentage
- **Active Subscribers**: Engaged contacts ready to receive emails
- **New This Month**: Recent subscriber additions
- **Daily Email Volume**: How many emails you've sent recently
- **Sending Limits**: Your current plan's email rate limits

### Managing Campaigns

The Campaigns page is where you create, schedule, and track your email marketing efforts.

<img
  className="rounded-lg shadow-md my-4"
  src="/images/screenshots/campaigns.png"
  alt="MailGreet Campaigns Page"
/>

- **All Campaigns**: View sent, draft, and scheduled campaigns
- **Performance Metrics**: Track recipients, open rates, and click rates
- **Quick Actions**: Create new campaigns with one click
- **Search & Filter**: Find campaigns quickly by name or status

### Subscriber Management

Manage your entire audience from the Subscribers page with powerful organization tools.

<img
  className="rounded-lg shadow-md my-4"
  src="/images/screenshots/subscribers.png"
  alt="MailGreet Subscribers Page"
/>

- **Subscriber Groups**: Organize contacts by category or interest
- **All Subscribers View**: See everyone with status and email validity
- **Segments**: Create dynamic lists based on subscriber attributes
- **Custom Fields**: Store additional information like company or preferences
- **Import & Export**: Easily move data in and out of MailGreet

### Email Templates

Design professional emails with our template system.

<img
  className="rounded-lg shadow-md my-4"
  src="/images/screenshots/templates.png"
  alt="MailGreet Templates Page"
/>

- **Template Categories**: Newsletter, Marketing, Events, Transactional, Custom
- **Block Builder**: Visual drag-and-drop email construction
- **HTML Editor**: Full code access for advanced customization
- **Template Library**: Start from pre-designed layouts or create from scratch
