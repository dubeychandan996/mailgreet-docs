## What Are Credits in MailGreet?

MailGreet uses a credit-based system for email and phone number verification. Credits are required to validate contact information and ensure high deliverability rates.

* **1 credit** is used for each email or phone number verification.
* For **bulk verifications**, the number of credits used equals the number of entries processed.
For example, verifying **1,000 emails or phone numbers** will consume **1,000 credits**.

<Info>

**Note**: For **single** or **multiple** email or phone verification, whether you provide valid or invalid emails or phone numbers. 1 credit will be used per single verification and for bulk verification, respective multiple credits will be used.

</Info>

<Info>

**Note**: When **new subscribers** are added, then the email is verified automatically upon signup. This verification **does not** consume **1 credit**.
</Info>

* When you first signup as a **FREE USER**, then there are **0 credits** available for Free User Account.
